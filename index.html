About Us

Welcome to Starlet

Established in the year 2017, Starlet LED Lighting Private Limited is recognized among the noteworthy Manufacturer and OEM Business Service of a broad assortment of LED Bulb, LED Down Light, LED Street Light, Tube Light, LED Spot Light, LED Flood Light, LED Strip Light, etc.

Founded in 2017 by Mr. Jitender Kumar Tyagi, The Starlet Group today is one of the leading manufacturer of Lighting with a turnover of INR 60 Crores.

Manufacturing of Flood Lights, Street Lights, Conceal Lights & Down Lights with in-house PCB, SMT, Driver ,Moulding & Die casting facility.

Supplier to most Prestigious Electrical Industries in India.

Benefits of LED

Efficient Energy Usage

LED Bulbs use 15% of the electricity of a standard halogen lamp LED bulbs generate the same light levels as halogen lamps

Complementary Saving

since LED lights do not overheat electricity bills from air conditioning and fun Usage will greatly dimnish The life span of LED product is 8-10 longer than standard bulbs

Durable Build Quality

LED's have no filaments therefore can withstand more shock than regular bulbs

Rapid Brightening

All LED lights on at 100% of thier intended brightness every single time.

Environment Friendly

LED lights are Designed to be 100% recyclable. LED lights have a life span equivalent to 25 incandescent light bulbs.
